Vireya Hybrids
 Click on any plant image to enlarge it and show its 2025 price.
All prices are also listed in our catalogue which you can download from the Catalogue page.

All plants are bushy, well shaped and hardened off ready for planting. The Vireyas are in hard 17 cm plastic pots, and heights excluding the container range from 20 to 40 cm depending on variety. Look in About Us for a photo of a plant ready for sale.

  ANATTA GOLD
 Lovely deep gold rippled petals with burnt orange shading in the throat. This gives the flower a glowing depth of colour in a tone not usually seen. Good consistent flowering. Nice green foliage on a sturdy plant. Height 1.2m.
  ARAVIR
 Beautiful crisp clear highly scented white flowers. Compact semi–spreading growth habit. Height 1m.
  BLONDE BOMBSHELL
 Large soft pinky apricot edged flowers with a soft yellow centre. Very free flowering. Growth habit is similar to Simbu Sunset. An excellent performer. Height 1.2m.
  BOBBY DAZZLER       Our own new hybrid release for 2025
 A sister seedling to Vireya 'Tickety Boo'. Lovely bright orange medium sized flowers on a good rounded compact shrub with good vigour. A good reliable flowering variety that always puts on a show, much like 'Tickety Boo' does. Height 1.2m
  BUTTERMAID
 Full rounded trusses of up to 22 pale yellow flowers with a light suffusion of orange. Very stunning when in full flower. Vigorous grower. Height 1.5m.
  CAMEO SPICE
 Large creamy apricot flowers spiced with a cinnamon fragrance, 4 to 6 per truss. Vigorous grower with bold felted leaves. Height 1.5m.
  CANDLELIGHT
 Lovely medium to large creamy yellow flowers on a medium sized shrub. A very attractive flower. Good vigorous growth. Height 1.2 metres.
  CAPTAIN SCARLET
 Large trusses of orange red lightly scented flowers on a strong growing shrub. Flowers well when young. Height 1.2m.
  CARILLON BELLS
 Dainty melon pink bells 4cm long hang in clusters on a tidy small leafed plant. Very pretty. Height 50cm.
  CHARMING VALENTINO
 Attractive foliage plant with a tidy habit. Rich salmon red flowers. Suitable for containers, baskets and ground growing. Height 1m.
  CHERRY GLOW       Released in 2013
 A stunning shrub, with hot pink flowers with white in the base and tube. A new introduction for us for 2012/13. Richard Currie hybrid. Height 1m.
  CHERRY PIE
 Large cherry pink flowers with an iridescent glow. Bold rounded foliage on a spreading well shaped bush that sits low to the ground. Height 1m
  CORAL CHANCE
 Bright coral orange delicate starry flowers on a vigorous bushy plant. Hardy and flowers well. Height 1.2m.
  CORAL FLARE
 Soft coral pink flowers on a rounded bush that sits low to the ground. Good all round performer. Height 1m.
  CORAL SEAS
 Beautiful coral orange flowers with a tinge of yellow in the throat. Strong bushy shrub. Consistent flowering from a young age. Height 1.2m.
  CRÈME DE LA CRÈME
 Large blooms of creamy flowers with an apricot eye and lightly frilled petals. Lovely dark green foliage. Height 1.5m.
  DAINTY BELLE       Our own hybrid released in 2022
 Beautiful clear red bell shaped flowers on a finer foliaged plant. A vigourous growing plant which flowers regularly. Height 1.2m.
  DAWN CHORUS
 Pink lobed flowers shading to a creamy yellow throat, delicate tonings. New growth has a distinctive red flush. Very showy. Compact shrub. Height 1.2m.
  DEVINE
 Lovely medium to large creamy toned flowers with pink flushing through the back of the flower and dainty flecks of pink, toned with rich cream in the throat of the flower. Good bold felted sturdy foliage on a tidy compact growing plant. A Richard Currie hybrid. Height 1.2m
  ELOUISE       Our own hybrid released in 2022
 This is a miniature vireya with dainty white flowers with a delicate pink flush on the edges of the petals. An excellent plant for a small garden, edging of a border or containers. Bright glossy green leaves give the plant a compact rounded shape. Height 80cm.
  FAIRY DANCER
 This one is a real miniature, with small leaves and small soft orange flowers. Ideal for basket, ponga pots or containers. Height 15cm.
  FELICITAS
 Crisp white star like tubular flowers with a frilled edge to the petals. Highly scented with a hint of pink in the throat. Good compact habit, does well in a container or basket. Height 1m.
  FIRST LIGHT
 This is much like 'Star Posy' although the foliage is a deeper green and of heavier texture. The flowers are also a deeper shade of pink. Lightly scented. Height 1m.
  FLASH DANCE
 Very large vivid red orange flowers with a softer yellow throat. Similar to the ever popular Tropic Glow but with larger flowers and more intense colour. Forms a large upright bush. Height 1.5m.
  FLASH HARRY       Our own hybrid released in 2008.
 Very vivid cherry pink scented flowers with a softer creamy yellow in the throat. Flowers young and flowers often. Has good constitution and forms a well rounded shrub with deep green foliage. A winner! Height 1.2m.
  FRAGRANTEUM
 Stunning very large highly scented white flowers with a tinge of pink in the throat. Large bold green foliage. Height 1.2m.
  GILDED SUNRISE
 These large golden yellow flowers of 7 to 8 per truss make for a striking display. Vigorous growth to a height of 1.5m.
  GOLDEN CHARM
 Good full truss of golden apricot flowers. This plant has red stems and coppery glossy foliage. Good compact bushy habit. Height 1.2m.
  GREERS PINK
 A vigorous hardy growing vireya which masses itself in soft pink flowers. Good rounded shaped bush and a reliable flower. Height 1.2m
  GWENEVERE
 Lovely white scented flowers with a hint of pink in the throat. Full rounded truss. Good shaped shrub with dark green foliage. Height 1.2m.
  HALOED GOLD
 Lovely large clear golden yellow flowers with a thin frilled edge of orange. Good vigour. Height 1.2m.
  HIGHLAND WHITE JADE
 A lovely white vireya with long tubular upright flowers with a hint of yellow, nicely scented. Each truss has up to 10 flowers, which gives a starburst effect. Good bushy habit. Height 1.2m.
  HONEY BLUSH       Our own new hybrid release for 2025
 A 'Cameo Spice' open pollinated hybrid. This has the same type of foliage as 'Cameo Spice' although in a more compact form. Lovely medium to large honey coloured flowers with a slight pink blush to the edges of the flowers. A good repeat bloomer. Height 1.2m
  HONEY STAR
 Soft honey apricot flowers on a neat tidy plant. Height 1m.
  HOT GOSSIP
 Large tubular flowers of intense pink, delicately scented. Handsome foliage with red midribs. Very striking. Vigorous but compact plant. Height 1m.
  JAVA LIGHT
 Beautiful large tropical vivid orange flowers. Bright glossy green foliage. Has good vigour and flowers well. Forms a well rounded shrub. Height 1.2m.
  JEAN BAPTISTE
 Beautiful large scented creamy white flowers, sometime showing a hint of pink and an extra set of petals in the centre of each flower. A compact shrub with glaucous foliage. Height 1.2m.
  JOHN WEST
 Rich salmon flowers blending to apricot cream in the throat. Bold strong foliage on a large shrub. Height 1.2m.
  JUST PEACHY
 Beautiful peachy apricot flowers in a full truss. Semi prostrate shrub, which makes it ideal for baskets or ponga pots. Height 1m.
  KISSES
 Pink lobed flowers shading to a cream throat. Compact tidy shrub. Very similar to 'Dawn Chorus'. Height 1.2m.
  KRAKATOA
 Large vigorous shrub. Glossy lighter green foliage similar to Captain Scarlet and Shepherds Warning. Large flowers in a burnt orange colour. Height 1.2m.
  LAVA FLOW
 Lovely bright orange flowers held in clusters. Vigorous grower with light green new growth, darkening with age. Height 1.2 metres
  LIPSTICK
 Attractive white flowers with pink frosting on the tips of the petals. Good deep green foliage with compact habit. Height 1m.
  LITTLE CRACKER
 Dainty bright orange red flowers on a low growing slightly spreading shrub. A good miniature for the front of the border. Height 40cm.
  LITTLEST ANGEL
 Clusters of red bells on a semi prostrate plant makes it ideal for a hanging basket. Height 30cm.
  LULLABY
 Soft pink tubular flowers ageing to white. Bushy growth habit with good vigour. Hardy. Height 1.2m.
  LULU
 Beautiful bicolour with creamy yellow throat and apricot lobes. Has good vigour and shape. Height 1.2m.
  MADONNA       Released in 2013
 A beautiful fragrant vireya with large trusses of frilly cream tinged apricot pink flowers. Medium sized bush with large leaves. Height 1.2m.
  MOONWOOD
 Beautiful creamy highly scented flowers on a low compact plant. Very similar in shape and size to Felicitas. Height 1m.
  NE-PLUS-ULTRA
 A good compact vigorous grower, one of the best reds. 8 to 10 medium sized flowers per truss. Glossy green foliage. Height 1m.
  OUR MARCIA
 Soft clear pink flowers with a paler throat. Good vigour with a bushy habit. Height 1.2m.
  PENDRAGON
 Good foliage on a medium sized bush. Flowers are of a medium size in maize yellow edged in orange red, up to 9 per truss. Very showy. Height 1.2m.
  PENNYWHISTLE
 A pretty little Vireya with tiny vivid orange flowers held in clusters of 3–5 flowers per cluster. Height 60cm.

 Bright pink scented flowers with a softer creamy yellow in the throat. Coppery red new growth, similar to Solar Flare. Forms a large bushy shrub. Height 1.3m.
  PLUM DROPS
 Certainly a different colour tone for a vireya. Deep wine to plum pink bells on a compact bush. Good glossy foliage. Height 1m.
  RAINBOWS END
 Vivid cherry pink flowers with a light creamy yellow throat. Lightly scented. Height 1.2m.
  RED MOUNTAIN
 Good compact grower with rich dark red bell shaped flowers. Vigorous and hardy grower. Height 1.2m.

 Medium sized rosy red flowers on a spreading plant with glossy foliage. Good rounded habit that sits low to the ground. Height 1m.

  SAXON GLOW
 Red flowers held in clusters that sit up above the foliage. Very neat compact habit. Repeat flowering. Can be used to make a small informal hedge. Height 80cm.
  SCARLET QUEEN
 Bold orange flowers on a strong glossy green foliaged shrub. Good vigour and shape. Height 1.5m.
  SCENTED SUN
 Stunning vivid yellow ruffled flowers, which sometimes have a gold tinge to them. Glossy foliage with a copper tinge. Tops when it comes to a yellow. Forms a good rounded shrub. Height 1.2m.

 Forms a well–rounded evenly clothed shrub. Lovely large salmon rose flowers with a yellow hue in the throat. An excellent performer. Hardy. Height 1.5m.
  SHEPHERDS WARNING
 Large scarlet orange flowers in a large rounded truss, lightly scented. Strong vigorous plant with light green foliage. Height 1.5m.
  SIMBU SUNSET
 Large showy flowers, orange lobes shading through to yellow in the throat. Always popular. Height 1.2m.
  SIMPLY RED       Our own hybrid released in 2022
 A very rounded compact Vireya with bright red flowers en masse. Excellent plant for a container, front of a garden or for edging a border. Repeat flowering. Height 75cm.
  SOLAR FLARE       Our own hybrid released in 1996.
 A compact easy to grow plant with its new bright copper foliage that matures to a deep green with red stems and mid ribs. The large flowers, up to 9 per truss, are a vivid clear pink with a rich scent of carnations. Height 1.2m.
