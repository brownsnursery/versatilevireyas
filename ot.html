  Buxus microphylla
 This is the Japanese box hedge that is a very quick grower, about twice as fast as English box. Its foliage has lovely lime green colour tones. Japanese box also has a very shiny leaf. It can be kept clipped into a small hedge for use around formal plantings but is also at home as a border hedge up to 1–2 metres high. Plant at 25 to 30cm spacings. Easy plant to grow, will grow in sun or shade. Hardy. Good sturdy plants grown in a 9cm tube..
  Camellia Setsugekka
 A very popular camellia to use as a hedging plant. Lovely single white flowers which start to open in the Autumn. Forms a good dense hedge with dark green foliage and can be trimmed to a height that you require.Please email us for availability, size and price.
  Clivia miniata Orange
 These are grown from a selection of our best orange hybrids. As these are seedling grown, the tones of orange will vary from softer shades through to deep orange shades. Some will have yellow throats and some will be straight colours. Good sturdy plants grown in 2.5 litre pots. Height 60cm.
  Clivia miniata Orange seedlings
 An easy plant to grow that prefers semi shade. Grows to perfection under the shade of trees. Forms strong clumps of agapanthus type foliage with large brilliant orange heads of bloom in winter and early spring. Good sturdy plants grown in a 9 cm tube. Height 60cm.
