Welcome to Browns Nursery.

We are a leading grower of Vireyas in New Zealand, with over 50 years of combined Vireya knowledge. We are both trained horticulturalists and our aim is to produce quality plants, which are accurately named, at an affordable price. We also aim to introduce new varieties as they become available, either our own or from other reputable sources.

Current images are for the plants in our 2023 mail order catalogue. Please send us an email with what you would like to order and your delivery address and we will get back to you with availability and freight costs.

You are always welcome to visit our nursery, however please contact us in advance to arrange a suitable time.

We hope you enjoy looking through our web site. Regards Pauline and David.
